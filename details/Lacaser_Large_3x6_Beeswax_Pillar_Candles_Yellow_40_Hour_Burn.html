Lacaser Large 3x6 Beeswax Pillar Candles

Lacaser's 3x6 beeswax pillar candles offer a beautiful and practical addition to your home. Each candle is made from single-origin beeswax sheets, sustainably harvested from trusted apiaries, delivering a premium and eco-conscious product.

The handrolled design ensures a rustic, natural look, preserving the honeycomb textures found in real hives. This makes each candle not just a lighting source, but a small piece of nature's art displayed in your living space.

With an unscented and smokeless burn, these candles provide a clean, warm glow that enhances your room's atmosphere without any harsh or chemical smells. Perfect for creating a cozy, welcoming environment at home.

Ideal for both everyday use and special occasions, these 3x6 pillar candles burn for 35 to 40 hours, offering long-lasting illumination without the need for frequent replacements. Their subtle natural honey scent adds a comforting aroma to any setting.

To maintain their shape and ensure optimal performance, store these candles in a cool, dry place. This way, they continue to deliver warmth, style, and a touch of nature whenever you light them.

Whether you're looking for a reliable light source, a decorative accent, or a moment of calm, these natural candles are the perfect choice for your home decor needs.
