Ner Mitzvah Beeswax Shabbat Candles

These beautifully crafted candles are designed to illuminate sacred moments with pure, natural light. Made in the USA, each Ner Mitzvah candle is composed of 100% beeswax and features braided cotton wicks that burn evenly and safely.

Why Choose Ner Mitzvah Candles?

  • Natural & Non-Toxic Beeswax: Safe for use in homes with children or those sensitive to chemicals, these candles offer a clean and pure burn without harmful emissions.
  • Hand-Dipped Construction: Ensures a smooth, even finish and a soft ivory-white color that adds a touch of elegance to any setting.
  • 4-Hour Burn Time: Provides long-lasting illumination without excessive dripping, making them convenient for daily use or special occasions.
  • Steady Flame: The braided cotton wicks create a consistent flame, enhancing both the visual and spiritual experience.
  • Perfect for Religious Use: Ideal for Shabbat, vigils, and other spiritual rituals, these candles bring a traditional and calming ambiance to your space.

Versatile & Meaningful

Whether you're using them for religious ceremonies or as a thoughtful gift, Ner Mitzvah candles combine beauty, quality, and a deep respect for tradition. They are also suitable for use in churches, temples, and even as emergency blackout candles, thanks to their reliable design and natural materials.
